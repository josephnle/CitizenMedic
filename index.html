Our mission is to empower everyone
to survive disasters.

We know that many people have an “out of sight, out of mind” attitude about what to do when the niceties of civilization we take for granted stop working. It’s a false sense of comfort that doesn’t change your likelihood of being hit by a power outage, earthquake, flood or hurricane.

Getting prepared is easier
and more affordable than you think

Throughout the workshops we’ve conducted, we know that one of the biggest impediments in getting people to act on their own personal disaster preparedness plan is that many view earthquake/flood/disaster prep with the binary notion that you’re either the bearded “prepper” guy living in the woods with 10,000 rounds of ammunition, surrounded by years of canned of food - or you are are normal person.

The truth is that there is an entire spectrum of preparedness - and our goal is to help you move one notch over to the right.

About us

Citizen Medic’s mission is to empower individuals & families at every income level to be more self-reliant in the face of natural disasters and offer informative tutorials to the public on improving their resilience in the face of unexpected (but statistically-likely during the course of a lifetime) interruptions to our lives through natural or man-made disasters.

Citizen Medic is the creation of Kai Chang, a longtime Disaster Preparedness speaker for the American Red Cross and CPR/First Aid instructor. He has taught life-saving skills to thousands over the years through the Red Cross - spearheading workshops attended by corporate executives, technology leaders, Boy Scouts/Girl Scouts and low-income community groups. As a resident of Napa, CA, he survived the 2017 Napa fires and his experiences have sharpened his passion to offer life-saving disaster preparedness advice for his community.

His signature presentation was an inspiring TEDx talk on his experiences in administering emergency care which you can find on YouTube.
